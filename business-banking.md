# Business Banking Basics

## Introduction
This document serves as a guide to setting up business banking for new ventures. It will cover the necessary steps, important considerations, and useful tips.

## Step 1: Choose the Right Bank
- Research different banks and credit unions to find the best fit for your business needs.

## Step 2: Gather Necessary Documentation
- Identification: Personal ID for all account holders.
- Business Entity Documents: Articles of incorporation, etc.
- Employer Identification Number (EIN).

## Step 3: Open Your Account
- Schedule an appointment or visit the bank's website to apply.
- Complete the required forms and provide the necessary documents.

## Conclusion
Having a dedicated business bank account is crucial for managing finances. Following these steps will aid in setting up your business banking effectively.
